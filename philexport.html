PhilExport Cebu Election 2019

Please be informed that PHILEXPORT Cebu will again be conducting itsAnnual Election of Board of Trustees for the term 2020-2021 on November 26, 2019 at 3:00 to 5:00pm at Philexport Cebu Office. Six (6) of the eleven Board of Trustees of PHILEXPORT CEBU will end their term by year end 2019.

The six (6) duly recognized Sectoral Associations that shall be affected by these regular turn-over of Board representation are:


FURNITURE SECTOR – 1 representation for two year term (2020-2021)


SHELLCRAFT SECTOR – 1 representation for two year term (2020-2021)


SEAWEEDS SECTOR – 1 representation for two year term (2020-2021)


MEPZCEM – 1 representation for two year term (2020-2021)


FOOD SECTOR – 1 representation for two year term (2020-2021)


HEALTH & WELLNESS SECTOR – 1 representation for two year term (2020-2021)

Deadline for submission of nominations to Philexport Cebu Office is on November 18, 2019 together with written acceptance of nominees

The candidates from these sectors shall yet be voted by the majority of Philexport Cebu members, in general (i.e. not only by the members belonging to your sector) in the upcoming elections, for them to sit in the Board.

To guide your choice for nomination please find the following guidelines:

1. On the person eligibility
  A. He/she must be of good moral character;
  A. He/she must be the officially recognized representative of a member company

2. On the company’s eligibility
  A. The said company must likewise be a member of good standing of Philexport Cebu.

3. Must be nominated by the Sectoral member or by a Regular Member of the same sector;

4. The nominated candidate must submit a written acceptance of his/her nomination.

Attached please find the nomination form and candidates acceptance to be filled up and faxed to Philexport Cebu at fax nos. 254-4333 and 254-0774 or email us at info@philexportcebu.org

Exporters' Powwow 2019

The Board of Trustees of Philexport Cebu, the umbrella organization of all exporting sectors in Region VII, requests the pleasure of your company on November 22, 2019 (Friday) at Marco Polo Grand Ballroom, Nivel Hills, Cebu City from 17.00h to 22.00h, to celebrate EXPORTERS’ POWWOW 2019 with the exporters.

The more than 380-strong export companies will be celebrating its annual “EXPORTERS’ POWWOW”, the fellowship portion of its general assembly meeting. This year’s theme is “Compete In all Markets”, which aims to inspire our exporters to consider other markets not affected by trade tensions such the US-China dispute, Hong Kong urest, the EU’s Brexit and Japan-Korea trade Issue.

Our keynote speaker this year is the President of Philexport National Mr. Sergio Ortiz-Luis, Jr.. He will talk about our plans and programs amid current global trends.

BOC Deputy Commissioner Edward Dy Buco, will also talk about the new EU requirement called REX System and EU’s ARISE spokesperson Atty. Paolo Vergano, will talk about their “Assist Project in ASEAN”.

Just like the previous “POWWOW’s” many exciting prizes and entertaining performance are in store for you. Please come in BLACK and/or WHITE attire.

Listening To The Vocie Of Customer (VOC) Workshop

OBJECTIVES

By the end of this one-day workshop project, the participants will be able to :

Understand and apply the tools needed to identify and prioritize the various parameters require by the their customers;
Understand and apply the use of an importance-perform (IP) survey and analysis to prioritize their customers’ requirement;
Identify the most important customers’ needs from the (IP) analysis;
Formulate the proper programs and interventions in order to increase satisfaction in their customers’ important needs;

Understand and apply the output from the Workshop into their existing “customer satisfaction” monitoring practices.
